How to play

Place an emoji in an empty cell. The reaction engine resolves nearby physical, chemical and biological effects. Make a line of three identical objects to win.

  • 💧 + 🔥 → 💨 water becomes steam while the fire is extinguished.
  • 💧 + 🌱 → 🌿 → 🌳
  • ⚡ + flammable objects → 🔥
  • 🦠 infects living objects.
